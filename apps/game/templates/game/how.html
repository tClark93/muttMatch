How Title

Mutt Match is just like a dating app - except for the awkward conversation, exit strategies, and possibility of getting murdered. Oh yeah, and your match is your best friend for life.

To match with an animal, simply hit like. If his description doesn't fit your lifestyle, you can hit pass. After liking, you can schedule a playdate right away, or have the option to look through more animals and schedule one later in your session. We email the shelter to let them know who you're seeing and what time you'll arrive!
